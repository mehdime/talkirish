    Any ideas on another language that uses similar letters or some other way to get around this?  I have tried copying and pasting the letters from another program but the recipients can't see the "offending" letters.

    I got my answer - Go raibh maith agaibh!

     

    If you hold down the vowel that you want a fada on, it will bring up a selection of accents to use on the letter.  Apparently, the issue was on the receipents' ends -- their phones could not "read" the fada vowels.

     

    Hopefully this helps other people who use an iPhone :)
