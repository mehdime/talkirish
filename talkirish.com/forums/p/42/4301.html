    Anita:

    "G "    ?

    GRÁ

    love

    grá tire

    love of country

     The next letter is " Á "

    Since Anita questioned why my choice was  G  instead of H the answer is because words in Irish starting with an H are limited in number, and since the site is based from the English language (most folks here seem to speak and understand it), here are a few examples of what my American tought mind thinks of when I try to come up with some word starting with an H: house, horse, hill, home, hot, hay and the same words in order in Irish are: teach, capall, cnoc, baile, te, féar. Anyway if a word you choose ends with an H you or the next person can choose between either of the last two letters of that word.
