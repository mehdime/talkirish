    Just released - Interactive Online Irish Course

    Reply | Contact

    So after a LOAD of hard work we've released the first 20 lessons of our interactive Irish lessons, based on the classic Buntús Cainte course. It's taken us longer than we expected but we think it was well worth it. Our course is gorgeous and we believe it's the best Irish language course ever published online. 

    We've taken the text and audio from the book course and we've commissioned fantastic new colour graphics, added grammar notes and quizzes, to create a fun new way to learn Irish. The 20 lessons should take you around 10 hours to complete - and give you a really solid introduction to the Irish language.

    You can learn more about the course here:

    http://talkirish.com/online-irish-course/

    You can find out what's covered in each individual lesson in our lesson list here:

    http://talkirish.com/forums/p/3751/6544.aspx#6544

    As usual, you can try before you buy - view the first lesson for free here:

    http://talkirish.com/online-irish-course/freelesson.aspx
