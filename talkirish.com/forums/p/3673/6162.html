    Is Buntús Cainte MP3 course available for download from the iTunes Store?

    Reply | Contact

    Unfortunately, Apple's restrictions on the iTunes store prevent us from offering this course directly from iTunes. Apple doesn't allow small publishers like us to publish directly to the iTunes store. We have to go through a third-party publishing company. And none of the third-party publishing companies we could find supported embedding lyrics in MP3 files. So instead, you can buy and download this course directly from us!
