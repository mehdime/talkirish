    Dia's Mhuire duit,

    I know what's happening - sorry I didn't get it before.

    When you enter the end of section quiz you are taken directly to question 2 because you've already been 'tested' earlier in the lesson with a question. The quiz review function tracks all questions in each lesson as being part of an overall quiz.

    In some lessons, there'll be more than one question before the final question set, in which case you'll be taken to question 3 or even 4 as the 'first' question of the final quiz set.

    It is as designed - but I can appreciate that it may look like a bug. Question is (ha ha) should I redesign it so that the questions in the lesson aren't tracked, so therefore aren't included in the end of lesson quiz numbering system? The downside of this is they then can't be reviewed by the learner. Or should I leave things are they are?

    Let me know what you think!
