    Greatwestroad,
    Thanks for your query - I've put our lovely designer on the job of creating a lesson list for free download on the site ASAP.
    The lesson list will take a while to build, as we're creating it along with a brief description of what's covered as part of our online course (have you seen the free lesson at http://talkirish.com/online_irish_course/ ?).

    When we release Buntús Cainte parts 2 and 3 for MP3, I'll release an updated version of part 1, with descriptive lesson titles. Of course we'll send anyone who has already purchased Buntús Cainte Part 1 a free download for Part 1, so you can simply upgrade. However, this won't be available until early 2010.
