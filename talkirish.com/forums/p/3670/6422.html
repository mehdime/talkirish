    Hi Kam,

    Fáilte chuig Labhair Gaeilge (welcome to Talk Irish). 

    Did you know you can listen to the audio on your iPod and see the lyrics at the same time? Like you, I'm a visual learner so I find hearing the audio and seeing the text is very important.

    http://talkirish.com/irishcourse/userguide.aspx

    I think my favourite way to learn is with text, audio and images - which is why I love our new course! You can try the first lesson online for free:

    http://talkirish.com/online_irish_course/

    My best advice to you on learning Irish is to let go of the thought that it's hard or difficult. That can be a really big block to learning. Instead focus on starting from the start - the very first Buntús Cainte lesson is a great place to start. And stay on that lesson until you've really cracked it. Then move on slowly after that.

    I know I try to go too fast with my Irish and get disheartened - and that's bad because so much of language learning is about confidence, enjoyment and fun!

    Tóg go bóg é (take it easy) and you'll get there quicker!

    Slán anois,

    Michelle
