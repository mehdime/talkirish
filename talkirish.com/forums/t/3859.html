    Dia duit,

    I have been learning Irish steadily for about six months and have started looking for new ways to do this. One way I have decided to do this is through a blog. In the blog I am going to essentially share the Irish I learn.

    I have started writing posts in English but I have decided that my aim will be to try and introduce Irish before finally writing all of my posts in gaeilge; my target is to be doing this within 18 months. Hopefully people will find it interesting as I live in London and I am excited to explore the Irish Language around my home city, hopefully I will meet some people and find some interesting places. 

    (I also have a keen interest in performing music, and my blog will also be documenting me learning some traditional Irish instruments)

    My blog address is,

    Please consider supporting me by reading blog and checking for updates (and most probably correcting me!),
