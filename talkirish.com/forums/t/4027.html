    I understand what you mean...although, to be fair, there are two alternative considerations.

    One is that the learning process is facilitated through repedition, so repeating them isn't necessarily a bad thing.  Although it does seem to limit us to 365 words.

    Secondarily, when new people join the site, they probably need that initial set of words.  I know I haven't mastered all of them yet.

    So maybe we need a "Set 2" of words that could be selected once you've been through the first group?

    Of course, there is the "Seanfhocal" section that provides some significant additions to the vocabulary and grammar, but sometimes idiomatic Irish doesn't leave you clear as to the meaning of the words within an expression....

    I back your basic premise that it would be great to have more words, but maybe Michelle can look at a way of doing that without losing the first set.
